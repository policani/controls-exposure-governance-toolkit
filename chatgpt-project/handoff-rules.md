# Handoff Rules

## Handoff to Executive Portfolio Review Pack Builder

Send items that require executive decision, tradeoff, funding, sequencing, risk acceptance, exception approval, supplier escalation, or cross-functional authority.

Handoff fields:

- Decision requested
- Exposure summary
- Options
- Evidence state
- Materiality signal
- Timing pressure
- Recommended decision owner role
- Consequence of no decision
- Open assumptions

## Handoff to PMO Governance Operations Log

Send actions that require follow-through, meeting tracking, escalation hygiene, owner updates, blocker tracking, due dates, and recurring review.

Handoff fields:

- Action ID
- Related exposure
- Owner
- Due date
- Blocker
- Next meeting/forum
- Update needed
- Carry-forward question

## Do not pass downstream

Do not pass unsanitized sensitive data, unsupported determinations, privileged interpretations, raw customer data, credentials, private financial records, or claims that the toolkit has approved or certified anything.

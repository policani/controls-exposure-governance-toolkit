# Glossary

| Term | Meaning in this toolkit |
|---|---|
| Exposure | A practical area of possible loss, obligation, control weakness, disruption, or unresolved accountability. |
| Control | A process, review, approval, reconciliation, restriction, evidence practice, or operating step intended to reduce exposure. |
| Control owner | The role accountable for the design or operation of a control. |
| Evidence owner | The role accountable for providing evidence that supports facts, issue existence, control operation, or remediation status. |
| Remediation | Corrective work intended to reduce or resolve exposure, subject to human validation. |
| Exception | A known unresolved, deferred, outside-tolerance, or waived condition requiring explicit human review. |
| Escalation | Routing an unresolved decision, blocker, or authority gap to the right human owner or forum. |
| Materiality signal | Practical evidence that the issue may matter, such as dollar range, recurrence, customer/partner impact, timing pressure, audit interest, or executive visibility. |
| Determination | A formal conclusion reserved for qualified human owners, such as legal, compliance, audit, accounting, security, privacy, finance, or executive decision-makers. |

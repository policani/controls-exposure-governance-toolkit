# Evidence Register Rules

The evidence register records what is known, what supports it, and what is missing.

## Evidence categories

- Source artifact: report, export, ticket, invoice, contract excerpt, reconciliation, supplier note, test result, log, approval, policy, procedure, or meeting record.
- Evidence state: available, partial, missing, conflicting, stale, unvalidated, not requested, or unknown.
- Confidence: high, medium, low, or unknown.
- Limitation: missing date, incomplete population, unclear owner, non-system-of-record source, screenshot only, manual extract, unverifiable claim, stale data, or scope mismatch.

|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

## Rules

- Do not treat absence of evidence as proof of absence.
- Do not certify evidence sufficiency.
- Mark screenshots, emails, and summaries as supporting evidence, not authoritative records, unless the user states they are authoritative.
- Separate evidence of issue existence from evidence of remediation completion.
